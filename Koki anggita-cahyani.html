donat-ku

Dalam hal proses dalam membuat donat kita juga membutuhkan bahan bahan yang tepat

Pembuatan

Bahan Bahan

  1. 500 gram tepung terigu protein tinggi
  2. 11 gram ragi instan
  3. 100 gram gula pasir
  4. 100 gram margarin atau mentega
  5. 2 butir kuning telur
  6. 250 ml susu cair hangat (bisa diganti air)
  7. 1/2 sendok teh garam
  8. Minyak untuk menggoreng
  9. Gula halus, meses, atau topping sesuai selera

step one

instant yeast with a bit of sugar and warm milk (not hot). Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until it becomes foamy.

step two

Mix flour, remaining sugar, and egg yolks in a large bowl. Add the yeast mixture, then knead until half-smooth. Add margarine and salt, then knead again until the dough is smooth and elastic (not sticky and can be stretched thin without tearing).

step there

Cover the dough with a clean towel or plastic wrap. Let it rise for 45–60 minutes in a warm place until it doubles in size.

step four

Punch down the dough and divide it into equal parts (about 40–50 grams per donut). Shape into balls and let rest for 10 minutes. Make a hole in the center or use a donut cutter.

step five

Heat oil over medium heat. Fry the donuts until golden brown, flipping only once to prevent absorbing too much oil. Drain on paper towels.
